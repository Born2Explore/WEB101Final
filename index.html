The Buildings at RVC

Building Name Acronym Description Opening and Closing Times Floors
Classroom Building 1 CL1 Here you can find gyms and workout studios, it's also where basketball games are held. 7:30am-10:15pm 2
Educational Resource Center ERC This building is where the library is located, you can also grab some coffee at Meg's cafe or visit Eagle support on the second floor. 7:30am-10:15pm 3
Student Center SC If you're hungry you can grab some food at Papa John's or Subway near the welcome desk, or go downstairs to visit the bookstore. On the upper floor you can find Records and Registration as well as Finacial Aid. 7:30am-8:15pm 3
Classroom Building 1 and 2 CL1 and CL2 These buildings are where the classrooms are held. You can find art and sculpting studios as well as computer labs, but mostly classrooms and offices. 7:30am-10:30pm 3
Woodward Technology Center WTC If you need to work on a computer or print something out, you can do so in the computer lab located in this building. It is also where the IT helpdesk is, as well as classrooms and offices. 7:30am-10:15pm 3
Karl J. Jacobs Center for Science and Math JCSM This building is where science and math are taught, hence the name. There are science and math labs available for students, as well as study areas. 7:30am-10:15pm 4
